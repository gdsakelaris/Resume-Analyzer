Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and we'll prorate the difference.

What happens if I exceed my limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. On Enterprise, overage is billed at $0.50 per additional candidate. On other plans, you'll need to upgrade.

Is there a free trial?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required for the Free plan.

How does AI scoring work?

We use GPT-4o to analyze resumes against your job description, scoring candidates on skills match, experience, and cultural fit.
